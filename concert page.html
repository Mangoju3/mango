EXPERIENCE A DIFFERENT KIND OF FESTIVAL

The hottest emerging artists and the stars you already love. And that’s just the  beginning. This is a party that completely takes over San Diego’s waterfront & downtown. Most festivals pull you out to deserted locations, whereas Wonderfront integrates one of America’s most vibrant downtowns right into the festival.

Life

All Along the Wonderfront

Where the City
Meets the Sea

Beautiful waterfront parks, piers, ships and yacht parties play host. More than 250 downtown bars, restaurants, breweries and hotels all within steps. Ferries between stages or to the beautiful beaches of Coronado Island. After-parties in the Gaslamp Quarter and Little Italy. Sunset cruises, ocean activities and jet boat rides. America’s craft beer capital, and one of the top culinary cities. All imagined into one Festival destination. Pop in and out of the festival grounds at any time with your RFID wristband, as we take over the town.
